Investment Mathematics Explained

1. Amortization Engine

A mortgage is paid back in Equated Monthly Installments (EMI). Each month, interest is calculated on the outstanding balance. The remaining portion pays down principal.

Principal Paid = Monthly EMI - Outstanding × r

2. Annualized IRR Math

IRR is the discount rate that sets the Net Present Value (NPV) of all cash flows to zero. Our model solves this using binary search root-finding.

0 = -DownPayment + Σ[CFₜ / (1+r)ᵗ]
